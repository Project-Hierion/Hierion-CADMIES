# CADMIES GUI Roadmap

## Current Focus (v1.0)
- ✅ Core concept management (add, browse, view)
- ✅ System audit trail
- ✅ Air-gapped operation

## Phase 1 (Next Release)
- **Domain Explorer** – Browse concepts organized by domain
- **Random Concept** – Surprise me button with history
- **Enhanced Search** – Search across all concept fields

## Phase 2
- **Statistics Dashboard** – Library analytics and charts
- **Timeline View** – Visualize concept creation over time
- **Export Tool** – Export concepts as JSON, Markdown, CSV

## Phase 3
- **Concept Map** – Visual graph of concept relationships
- **Comparison Tool** – Side-by-side concept comparison
- **Batch Import** – Preview and import from conversation files

## Phase 4
- **System Health** – Integrity checking and repair tools
- **Relationships Editor** – Manually link concepts
- **Featured Concept** – Curated concept of the week

## Future Possibilities
- Optional secure sharing (sandboxed, user-consent only)
- Mobile-responsive design
- Plugin architecture
