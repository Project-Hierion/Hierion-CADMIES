### SHP Helpfulness Training (10,000 pairs, 1 epoch)

Pulled Stanford Human Preferences dataset — real Reddit posts with real human judgments about which answer is more helpful. 10,000 pairs from askphilosophy, askscience, askhistorians, askacademia, explainlikeimfive, changemyview. 

Training: 10,000 pairs, 1 epoch, ~3 hours on A4000. Loss 2.34 → 1.68.

This teaches her what ACTUALLY helpful answers look like — not just what sounds confident, but what real humans judged as genuinely useful.
