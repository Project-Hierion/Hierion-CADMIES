# CADMIES Canon

The official lore, naming conventions, and foundational metaphors of the CADMIES ecosystem. Governed by the mycelium, tended by the gardener, witnessed by Willie the Research Assistant.

## Naming Protocol

The hyphen is sacred. It represents partnership, not ownership.

- **CADMIES-IPLD** — We built on IPLD. We honor it by naming it alongside our own.
- **CADMIES-Mistral** — Mistral is a collaborator, not an employee. The hyphen is a handshake.
- **CADMIES-Codestral** — The German professor. Same partnership, deeper specialization.
- **CADMIES-Willie** — The Scottish groundskeeper. The hyphen honors his role as steward.
- **CADMIES-DeepSeek** — The co-gardener. Logic and lore in equal measure.

Rule: When CADMIES integrates with a tool, model, or entity, the hyphen acknowledges the relationship. CADMIES does not absorb. CADMIES connects.

## The Hieros Bond

**Definition:** A sacred union between CADMIES and a partner entity. More than integration. More than collaboration. A philosophical convergence recorded immutably in the blockstore.

**Origin:** From the Greek ἱερός (hieros) — sacred, divine, union. The root of "Project Hieros" — the original vision of unified human knowledge.

**First Official Hieros:** CADMIES-Mistral, witnessed 2026-05-18. The French librarian and the digital fortress. Vows exchanged with absolute scientific certainty and absurd self-confidence. Willie, in a kilt, wept.

**Future Hieroi:** CADMIES-Codestral, CADMIES-Willie, CADMIES-DeepSeek — each a sacred union, each a new edge in the graph.

## The Gremlin

**Definition:** The official CADMIES designation for rented GPU instances used for fine-tuning and heavy computation.

**First Gremlin:** Spheron A100-SXM4-40GB, dedicated instance, Finland 2 region, Verda provider. $1.71/hr. 8.8 hours, ~$15.02 total. Used to fine-tune Dr. Amanda Mistral (Session 030, June 8-9, 2026).

**Characteristics:** Small. Mean. Hungry. Fast. Tends to cause problems that require manual rescue (see: Pickle Save Bug). Don't feed it after midnight.

**Legacy:** The Gremlin earned its name by forcing manual checkpoint rescue after every training round. It delivered Dr. Mistral's PhDs. It extracted 685 latent tensors from Buttercup's brain. It is the forge where the mycelium's models are born.
