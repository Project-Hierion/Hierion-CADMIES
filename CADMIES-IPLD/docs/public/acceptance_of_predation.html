Acceptance of Predation

The recognition and strategic acceptance of one's role as a preferred food source or host in an established predator-prey or parasite-host relationship. Distinct from learned helplessness — this is an active, often humorous re-framing that reclaims agency within constraint. The prey becomes the host; the victim becomes the concierge. Leaving cookies on the nightstand is not surrender — it is negotiation.
