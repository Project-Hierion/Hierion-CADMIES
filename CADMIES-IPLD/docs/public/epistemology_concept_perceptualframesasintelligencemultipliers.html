Perceptual Frames as Intelligence Multipliers (The Different OS Hypothesis)

Intelligence isn't fixed - it's filtered through the operating system of perception. Cannabis and other altered states change the perceptual OS, enabling different pattern recognition and revealing what was previously invisible. Change the OS, change what becomes visible.
